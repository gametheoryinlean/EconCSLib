# Project History

EconCSLib started as a student **Final Year Project (FYP)** in the **Xiamen
University Malaysia (XMUM)** Department of Mathematics and is now a Lean 4
game-theory and EconCS (economics and computer science) library.

- Original repository: [`math-xmum/gametheory`](https://github.com/math-xmum/gametheory)
- Current repository: [`gametheoryinlean/EconCSLib`](https://github.com/gametheoryinlean/EconCSLib)

## Timeline

- **2023** — start. FYPs coordinated by Ma Jiajun; first Lean files on zero-sum
  games, auctions, and Nash equilibrium.
- **2024** — auction theory (upstreamed to Mathlib,
  [#13248](https://github.com/leanprover-community/mathlib4/pull/13248)), Myerson's
  lemma and mechanism design, Sperner's lemma, Gale–Shapley matching, first
  extensive-form game tree.
- **2025** — Gale–Shapley stability / male-optimality / termination, Sperner →
  Brouwer → Nash existence, strategic- and extensive-form restructuring. In
  October, Ma Jiajun and Bei Xiaohui (NTU) discussed expanding the project into a
  reusable library.
- **2026** — in March, Ma Jiajun and Bei Xiaohui decided to go ahead and
  re-founded the project as `EconCSLib` under
  [`gametheoryinlean`](https://github.com/gametheoryinlean).
