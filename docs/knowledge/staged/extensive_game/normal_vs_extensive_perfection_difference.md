# Normal And Extensive Perfection Can Differ

MFoGT Section 6.6 gives examples showing that normal-form perfection and
extensive-form perfection are distinct refinements.

- In the game of Figure 6.18, the indicated profile is extensive-form perfect but
  not perfect in the associated normal form.
- In the game of Figure 6.19, the indicated profile is perfect in the normal form
  but not perfect in the extensive form.

The text also notes that some games have empty intersection between the two sets
of refinements.
