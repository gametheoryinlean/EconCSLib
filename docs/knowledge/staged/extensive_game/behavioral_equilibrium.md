# Behavioral Equilibrium

Let $\Gamma$ be a finite extensive form game with perfect recall. A mixed
equilibrium of $\Gamma$ is a Nash equilibrium of its mixed normal-form extension.
A behavioral equilibrium of $\Gamma$ is a Nash equilibrium of the compact
continuous game whose strategy sets are behavioral strategy spaces $B_i$ and whose
payoffs are expected payoffs in $\Gamma$.
