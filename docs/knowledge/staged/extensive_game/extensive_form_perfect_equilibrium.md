# Extensive Form Perfect Equilibrium

An extensive form perfect equilibrium of a perfect-recall extensive form game
$\Gamma$ is a perfect equilibrium of its agent normal form $\Gamma^a$.

This refinement applies normal-form perfection at the level of information-set
agents rather than only to the original players' reduced normal form.

## References

- [MFoGT, Def. 6.6.3] Laraki, Renault, and Sorin, *Mathematical Foundations of Game Theory*. Extensive form perfect equilibrium is perfection in the agent normal form.
