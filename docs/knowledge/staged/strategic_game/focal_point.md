# Focal Point

A focal point, also called a Schelling point, is a Nash equilibrium that players
coordinate on in the absence of communication because it is natural, special, or
salient to them.

MFoGT notes that symmetric equilibria or Pareto-dominating equilibria can act as
natural focal points, but focal-point selection may conflict with best-response
invariance.
