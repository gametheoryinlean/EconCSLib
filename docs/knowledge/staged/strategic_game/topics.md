# Staged Strategic Games Topic Catalog

Canonical folder topic: `strategic_games`

## Subtopics

- `strategic_games.core` - strategic games, profiles, deviations, best responses, and feasible payoffs.
- `strategic_games.equilibrium` - Nash equilibrium, existence, rationalizability, and computation examples.
- `strategic_games.dominance` - dominance, iterated elimination, and dominance-solvable games.
- `strategic_games.correlated` - correlated strategies, correlation devices, recommendations, obedience, and mediated games.
- `strategic_games.learning` - no-regret learning, Hannan sets, calibration, and convergence to correlated-equilibrium targets.
- `strategic_games.bayesian` - Bayesian games, type-contingent strategies, Bayesian equilibrium, and agent normal forms.
- `strategic_games.refinements` - perfect, proper, strict, epsilon-perfect, and epsilon-proper equilibria.
- `strategic_games.continuous` - compact, convex, quasi-concave, supermodular, monotone, discontinuous, and Reny-style games.
- `strategic_games.payoff_geometry` - threat points, prudent strategies, and payoff-set geometry.
- `strategic_games.population` - population games, Wardrop equilibria, and nonatomic variational characterizations.
- `strategic_games.variational` - general evaluation equilibria and variational-inequality formulations.
- `strategic_games.potential` - exact and ordinal potential games and potential-based equilibrium results.
- `strategic_games.manifold` - equilibrium manifolds, essential components, degree, and genericity.
- `strategic_games.dynamics` - Nash fields, replicator dynamics, and positive-correlation dynamics.
- `strategic_games.evolution` - ESS, NSS, invasion barriers, and replicator stability.
- `strategic_games.repeated` - repeated games, payoff evaluations, folk theorems, stochastic games, signals, and incomplete information.

## Boundary

Use `strategic_games`, not `strategic_game`, for new ids and references.
