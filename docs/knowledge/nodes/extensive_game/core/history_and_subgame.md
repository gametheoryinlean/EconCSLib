# History And Subgame

The history of a position $p$ is the finite sequence of predecessors from the
origin to $p$. The subgame $G[p]$ is the extensive game whose origin is $p$ and
whose tree is the subtree generated by all successors of $p$, with the inherited
player assignments and terminal payoffs.
