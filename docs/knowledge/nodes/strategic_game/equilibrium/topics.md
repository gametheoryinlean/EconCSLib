# Accepted Equilibrium Topic Catalog

Canonical folder topic: `equilibrium`

## Scope

`game_theory.strategic_game.equilibrium` collects existence and structural
results about Nash equilibria of finite normal-form games: the (mixed) Nash
equilibrium concept, ε-equilibria, existence via Brouwer's fixed-point theorem,
symmetry, individual rationality, and semialgebraic structure of the
equilibrium set.

## Boundary

Equilibrium refinements (perfect, proper, strict) belong in
`game_theory.strategic_game.refinements`; continuous-game existence belongs in
`game_theory.strategic_game.continuous`; correlated equilibria in
`game_theory.strategic_game.correlated`.
